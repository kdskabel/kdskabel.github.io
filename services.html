Services

Dirt and Debris Removal

Using our power broom, we first go over your turf to bring up all of the dirt and debris to the top of the surface. We then use our turf vacuum to collect all of the dirt and debris.

Pet and Odor Elimination

We specialize in ridding your turf of pet waste and odors. To eliminate odors, we use a hydrogen-peroxide based cleaner that is specifically designed to work with artificial grass. Completely safe and natural, this cleaner will make your turf smelling brand new!

Turf Restoration

Over the years, your turf will need some maintenance. Sometimes you may need to replenish your infill or you may need to restretch your turf. Whatever your turf may need, we will work with you to completely bring your turf back to life!
